What We Offer

Our Services

Residential and commercial plumbing and heating services across Essex, North London and Dartford. All work carried out by Gas Safe registered engineers.

🛁
01
Bathroom Renovations
Full bathroom transformations. We design, supply and install — from freestanding baths to walk-in showers, bespoke tiling, underfloor heating, and premium fixtures.
🍳
02
Kitchen Renovations
Complete kitchen plumbing including new sink installation, appliance hookups, full pipework, and waste systems to suit your dream kitchen layout.
🔥
03
Boiler Repair & Installation
Gas Safe registered engineers for all boiler makes and models. We diagnose, repair, service and install — including Vaillant, Worcester Bosch, and Ideal boilers.
🚿
04
Water Heater Services
Installation, repair and maintenance of water heaters and hot water cylinders. Efficient systems to reduce energy bills and ensure reliable hot water.
💧
05
Water Piping Repair & Install
Leak detection, pipe replacement, and full pipework installation for new builds and renovations. We work with copper, plastic and specialist pipe systems.
🔧
06
Leaks & General Repairs
Troubleshooting and repair of leaks, blocked drains, faulty taps, running toilets, and sink clogs. Fast response to minimise disruption and water damage.
📊
07
Water & Gas Meters
Meter installation, relocation, and connection work. We work alongside suppliers to ensure safe, compliant meter setups for residential and commercial properties.
♨️
08
Steam Services
Specialist steam system installation and repair for residential steam rooms and commercial applications. Full pressure testing and certification provided.
⚡
09
24hr Emergency Callout
Burst pipes, boiler breakdowns, severe leaks — we respond fast. Available 24 hours, 7 days a week for genuine plumbing and heating emergencies.
